# Wasteland Hustle

A simple asteroid mining clicker game where you collect minerals by clicking and upgrade your equipment to mine faster. As you progress, you can unlock tools like lasers, crew members, and automated drill bots to increase both manual and automatic mining speed, aiming to reach your resource goal as efficiently as possible.

---

## How to Play

- Click the main button to collect resources
- Earn points based on your current points per click
- Spend resources on upgrades to improve your efficiency
- Unlock new abilities like passive income and bonuses
- Reach the score goal to win the game

---

## Features

- Incremental click-based gameplay
- Upgrade system to increase points per click
- Planned/implemented passive income (auto collectors)
- Visual feedback with floating score indicators
- Sound effects for player interaction
- Win condition system

---

## Technologies Used

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript

---

## Goal

- Reach the required score as efficiently as possible by balancing manual clicking and upgrades.
